Chicken Meat

Home About Chicken Meat Pilau

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Salt
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Cooking oil
  • Spices (optional: curry, paprika)

Instructions

  1. Clean and cut the chicken into desired pieces.
  2. In a pot, heat oil and fry onions until golden.
  3. Add tomatoes, garlic, and ginger. Cook until soft.
  4. Add chicken and spices. Stir well.
  5. Cover and simmer until chicken is fully cooked.
  6. Serve hot with rice, chapati, or ugali.

Nutritional Info (Estimated)

Protein-rich, good source of iron and essential vitamins.

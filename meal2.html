Pilau

Home About Chicken Meat Pilau

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Beef or chicken (optional)
  • Onions
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Pilau masala spice
  • Salt
  • Cooking oil
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and fry onions until golden brown.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, and pilau masala. Stir for a minute.
  3. Add meat (if using) and cook until tender.
  4. Add rice and salt, then mix well with the spices.
  5. Add water, bring to a boil, then reduce heat and cover.
  6. Simmer until rice is cooked and water is absorbed.
